Cuisine

North Indian cuisine is a delightful blend of aromatic spices, flavorsome curries, and mouthwatering sweets. The region's culinary repertoire is famous worldwide and has won the hearts of food lovers everywhere.

From the iconic Butter Chicken and savory Biryani to the delectable assortment of bread (roti) varieties, North Indian food offers a tantalizing experience for the taste buds.

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular North Indian dish that features succulent pieces of grilled chicken simmered in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. It is best enjoyed with buttery naan bread or steamed rice.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, long-grain basmati rice, and contain chicken. It is a complete meal on its own and is often served with raita (yogurt dip) and papadum.

Rasgulla

Rasgulla is a classic North Indian dessert made from soft, spongy cheese balls soaked in sugar syrup. It is a delightful treat that is often served during festivals and special occasions.
