Culture

North Indian culture is a tapestry of vibrant festivals, deep-rooted traditions, and diverse customs. The region is known for its colorful attire, rich folklore, and classical art forms that have mesmerized audiences for centuries.

From the resplendent beauty of Kathak dance to the soul-stirring melodies of Hindustani classical music, North India boasts a cultural heritage that is both captivating and unique.
